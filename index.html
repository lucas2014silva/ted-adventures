TED GOES SHOPPING - PART 1

Ted was excited for his deliver when his phone rang it was the chocolates store telling him that the trucks that was going to deliver chocolates had an accident and all the chocolates fell into the river then he decided to buy a submarine to go underwater to go get the chocolates but when he got there with his submarine the chocolate had melted and he call all his friends to make a big hot chocolate party.
